Professional Code of Conduct

1. Preamble: The Foundation of Service

This Professional Code of Conduct defines the standards of behavior required of all individuals representing The Parishelter Foundation. Our mission to elevate humanity requires unwavering commitment to integrity, grace and accountability.

2. Integrity in Interaction
  • Dignity and Grace toward every beneficiary, colleague and partner.
  • Maintain clear professional boundaries at all times.
  • All interactions, especially involving children, must occur in open and transparent settings.
3. Ethical Stewardship of Power
  • Zero tolerance for Sexual Exploitation and Abuse.
  • All conflicts of interest must be disclosed immediately.
  • Support must be delivered based solely on need.
4. Digital and Media Ethics
  • Written informed consent required before using photos, videos or testimonials.
  • No exploitative imagery.
  • Strict confidentiality of all data.
5. Safety and Child Protection
  • The best interests of the child are paramount.
  • Any suspected harm or misconduct must be reported immediately.
6. Compliance and Accountability

Violation of this code may result in disciplinary action, termination of association, and referral to legal authorities where necessary.

Reporting Channel: concerns regarding conduct should be submitted to the independent monitoring team at elevate@parishelter.org

Safeguarding & Child Protection Policy

1. Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a safe and respectful environment for everyone we work with. Protection of children and adults at risk is a fundamental human right.

2. Scope of Application
  • Board Members
  • Executive Leadership
  • Staff
  • Volunteers
  • Interns
  • Consultants and Partners
3. Protection Standards
  • Zero tolerance for abuse, exploitation or neglect.
  • Child safety remains central to all programs.
  • Two-Adult Rule applies in all interactions with minors.
  • Safe recruitment and background checks required.
4. Mandatory Reporting & Response

All safeguarding concerns must be reported immediately. Initial assessments begin within 24–48 hours.

Privacy & Data Protection Policy

We are committed to protecting privacy and handling personal information with dignity, respect and transparency.

  • Information is collected only for support, volunteering, partnership or service coordination.
  • Data is accessible only to authorized personnel.
  • We do not sell or trade personal information.
  • Stories, images or testimonials require explicit consent.
  • Data is securely stored and deleted when no longer required.

Non-Discrimination & Inclusion Policy

Our commitment to elevating humanity is rooted in the belief that no life’s potential should be limited by the circumstances of birth.

Universal Equality
  • No discrimination based on race, tribe, ethnicity, gender or age.
  • No discrimination based on religion, political affiliation or creed.
  • No discrimination based on disability or socioeconomic background.
Expected Behavior

All representatives must promote inclusion, avoid discriminatory language and ensure fair access to Foundation opportunities.
